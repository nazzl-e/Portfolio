About Me

Hi, I'm Max Nazzle Fabella — full-time Video Editor & Freelance Content Producer based in the Philippines.

I specialize in direct-response video ads for Meta and Instagram, built to stop the scroll and drive real results. I work within production teams alongside scriptwriters and social media managers to deliver end-to-end ad campaigns.

On the freelance side, I help content creators, DTC brands, and businesses produce compelling video content — from hook to CTA. My workflow blends human storytelling with AI-assisted production tools to move faster without sacrificing quality.

When I'm not in the timeline, I'm deep in electronics — building ESP32 sensor dashboards and exploring embedded systems. I love solving problems whether on screen or on a circuit board.
